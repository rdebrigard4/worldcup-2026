# Icon credits

The home-screen / app icon (`public/apple-touch-icon.png`, `icon-192.png`,
`icon-512.png`, `emblem.svg`) is derived from the official 2026 FIFA World Cup
emblem.

- Source: [File:2026 FIFA World Cup emblem.svg](https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:2026_FIFA_World_Cup_emblem.svg), Wikimedia Commons
- License: Creative Commons Attribution-ShareAlike 4.0 International (CC BY-SA 4.0)
- The emblem is a trademark of FIFA; used here for a personal, non-commercial tracker.

The PNGs are the emblem centered on the app's `#0b1220` background. For the
dark tile the emblem is reversed out: the "26" digits and ™ are white, and the
"FIFA" wordmark is filled with the tile navy so it knocks out of the white "6"
(mirrors the official light-background version, where the digits are dark and
FIFA is reversed white).
